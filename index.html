Yuzhny Hospital Launches "Vital Sign" Initiative to Fortify City-Wide Emergency Response

By YH News Staff | Published June 22, 2025

YUZHNY â€” In a proactive move to enhance urban resilience, Yuzhny Hospital has officially launched "Operation Vital Sign," the most comprehensive emergency response drill in the city's history. The initiative, led by Deputy of Chief Dr. Glorious Gogoi, aims to stress-test the collaborative abilities of the city's police, military, and medical services in the face of a large-scale simulated disaster.

"Readiness is not a goal; it's a constant state of practice," Dr. Gogoi stated at the launch event. "Operation Vital Sign is designed to identify our strengths and, more importantly, to uncover our weaknesses in a controlled environment. When a real crisis hits, we must be able to work as one seamless unit. This drill will ensure we are prepared for that day."
