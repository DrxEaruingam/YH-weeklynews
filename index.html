Yuzhny Hospital Launches “Vital Sign” Initiative

Published June 22, 2025 by YH News Staff

Yuzhny — In a proactive move to enhance urban resilience, Yuzhny Hospital has officially launched “Operation Vital Sign,” the most comprehensive emergency response drill in the city's history.

Led by Deputy of Chief Dr. Glorious Gogoi, the initiative aims to stress-test the collaborative abilities of the city's police, military, and medical services in the face of a large-scale simulated disaster.

"Readiness is not a goal; it's a constant state of practice," said Dr. Gogoi. "This drill will ensure we are prepared for any crisis."
